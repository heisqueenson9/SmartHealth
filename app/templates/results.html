Dataset Scaling

All clinical inputs are pre-normalized to a standard unit scale (0-1) to ensure feature equitable impact during gradient-based training.

Pre-loading

Models are serialized via Joblib and pre-loaded on server startup to minimize predictive latency for the user interface.

Evaluation Rigor

Final selection was predicated on maximizing the Weighted F1-Score, ensuring reliability even in under-represented classes.

Selection Rationale

Random Forest was selected as the engine default for its superior ability to avoid overfitting in high-dimensional biological data.
